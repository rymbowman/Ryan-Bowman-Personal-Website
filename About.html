About Me

Hey! Thanks for visiting my website! I believe trust is one of the foundational pillars of any working relationship. To help build that foundation, let me share a bit about who I am.

My Background

The most important aspect of my life is my Christian faith. It guides my decisions and shapes my purpose in life. My family also plays a central role in who I am. I have been married to my wonderful wife, Carly, since May 2022, and we live in a suburb of Philadelphia alongside family and friends. This spring of 2024, we welcomed a special addition to our family, our pup Brody!

My Interests

I am passionate about traveling and a huge fan of Philadelphia sports! My five years as a high school teacher have given me the unique opportunity to travel during the summers. Some of my favorite places I have visited include Glacier National Park, Volcanoes National Park, Zion National Park, the Great Wall of China, northern Thailand, and the western coast of California. These experiences have left me with unforgettable memories and a deep appreciation for diverse cultures and landscapes.

My Journey to Software Development

So, why software development? This field excites me because it blends creativity with logical problem-solving. I thrive on the challenge of breaking down complex problems and building effective solutions. Over the past five years, I have worked as a high school history teacher, where I developed strong skills in problem-solving, analytical thinking, project management, adaptability, and teamwork. These experiences, along with my time running my own landscaping company and my love for solving puzzles, have revealed where my strengths and interests truly lie.

What's Next?

I am eager to apply my skills in software development, contribute to meaningful projects, and continue growing in this dynamic field. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to sharing my journey with you and building new connections!
